Mining is bad!March 14th, 2009
Mining is not good. Why?
The short answer: it's bad for the enviornment
However, heres the long answer.
First of all, mining has different ways
Every single way to mine has a downside.
Pretty much every way leads to side effects like:
 
erosion
sinkholes
deforestation
loss of biodiversity
significant use of water
dammed rivers and ponded waters
wastewater disposal issues
acid mine drainage
and contamination.
 
Are we stuck with these?
Not yet, we can minimize mining!
We can recycle to get materials.
Cheaper, but more time consuming.
 
Think about it. RDGFB out.
